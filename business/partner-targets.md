# partner-targets.md

## Partner Integration Roadmap for Coordination-Watchdog

### Target Integrations

1. **Zapier**
   - **Rationale**: Enables users to connect Coordination-Watchdog with thousands of apps for automated workflows, enhancing the tool's usability in diverse environments.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 5 Zaps, 100 tasks/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Simplifies user job of automating responses to coordination failures across various platforms.

2. **Slack**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with Slack allows real-time notifications and alerts for coordination failures, enhancing team responsiveness.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 10 integrations, 5GB storage.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Improves communication and immediate action on detected issues, streamlining team operations.

3. **Trello**
   - **Rationale**: Provides a visual task management interface to track coordination issues and resolutions, making it easier for teams to manage workflows.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited boards, lists, cards; 10MB attachment limit.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Helps users organize and prioritize responses to coordination failures visually.

4. **AWS CloudWatch**
   - **Rationale**: Offers powerful monitoring capabilities for AWS-hosted multi-agent systems, allowing for deeper insights and alerts on coordination failures.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 10 custom metrics, 1 million API requests/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Large (L)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances monitoring capabilities, providing users with a comprehensive view of system health and performance.

5. **Google Cloud Pub/Sub**
   - **Rationale**: Facilitates real-time messaging between services, enabling better coordination and handling of failures in multi-agent systems.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 10GB of data processed/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Supports users in building resilient systems by ensuring timely communication between agents.

6. **Microsoft Teams**
   - **Rationale**: Similar to Slack, integrating with Teams allows for seamless communication and alerts regarding coordination failures.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: Unlimited chat messages, 2GB file storage per user.
   - **Integration Effort**: Small (S)
   - **Value-Add**: Enhances team collaboration and response times to issues, improving operational efficiency.

7. **Asana**
   - **Rationale**: Integrating with Asana allows users to create tasks automatically when coordination failures are detected, streamlining issue resolution processes.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 15 users, basic task management features.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Helps users manage and track resolution efforts for coordination issues effectively.

8. **Twilio**
   - **Rationale**: Enables SMS and voice alerts for critical coordination failures, ensuring users are informed even when not actively monitoring the system.
   - **Free-tier Limits**: 500 SMS messages/month.
   - **Integration Effort**: Medium (M)
   - **Value-Add**: Provides immediate notification capabilities, allowing users to act swiftly on critical failures.

### Prioritization Criteria
- **Affiliate/Revenue-Share Potential**: Focus on partners that offer affiliate programs or revenue-sharing opportunities to enhance monetization strategies.
- **User Job Relevance**: Target integrations that directly address user pain points related to coordination failures and improve overall system reliability.
- **Integration Complexity**: Balance between ease of integration and the value added to the user experience.
